I write art criticism. My essays move between exhibitions, institutions, and the questions that linger after leaving a room, about what an artwork asks of its viewer, and what the viewer brings in return.

The work lives here and on Instagram at @behindartscenes, where I keep shorter notes from galleries, art fairs, and the spaces between.
